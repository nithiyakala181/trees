🌳 My 5 Favorite Trees 🌳

1. Banyan Tree

Speciality: National tree of India. It has the largest canopy and can live for 1000+ years.

Uses: Leaves used for plates, bark used in medicine, provides huge shade.

2. Peepal Tree

Speciality: Releases oxygen 24 hours. Sacred tree in Hinduism and Buddhism.

Uses: Leaves used in pooja, bark treats asthma, purifies air.

3. Coconut Tree

Speciality: Called "Kalpavriksha" - tree of life. Every part is useful.

Uses: Coconut water for drinking, oil, coir, leaves for roofing, wood for furniture.

4. Neem Tree

Speciality: Natural antibiotic. Keeps pests away.

Uses: Leaves for skin problems, twigs as toothbrush, oil as pesticide, medicine.

5. Teak Tree

Speciality: King of timber. Wood is termite resistant and very strong.

Uses: Used for making furniture, doors, boats, and house construction.
