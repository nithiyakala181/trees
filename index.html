🌳 My 5 Favorite Trees 🌳

1. Banyan Tree

Speciality: Largest canopy, lives for hundreds of years. National tree of India.

Uses: Shade, shelter, Ayurveda medicine.

2. Peepal Tree

Speciality: Releases oxygen 24 hours, sacred tree.

Uses: Pooja leaves, medicinal bark.

3. Coconut Tree

Speciality: "Kalpavriksha" - Every part is useful.

Uses: Water, oil, coir, roofing.

4. Teak Tree

Speciality: Very strong wood, water resistant.

Uses: Furniture, doors, boats.

5. Neem Tree

Speciality: Natural antibiotic, air purifier.

Uses: Medicinal leaves, neem oil.
