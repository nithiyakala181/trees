🌳 My 5 Favorite Trees 🌳

1. Banyan Tree

Speciality: It has the largest canopy and can live for hundreds of years.

Uses: Provides shade, shelter to birds, and used in Ayurveda.

2. Peepal Tree

Speciality: Releases oxygen 24 hours and is a sacred tree.

Uses: Leaves used in pooja, bark used for medicine.

3. Coconut Tree

Speciality: Every part of this tree is useful - "Kalpavriksha".

Uses: Coconut water, oil, coir, leaves for roofing.

4. Teak Tree

Speciality: Very strong wood that doesn't get damaged by water.

Uses: Used for making furniture, doors, and boats.

5. Neem Tree

Speciality: Natural antibiotic and air purifier.

Uses: Medicinal leaves, neem oil, toothbrush sticks.
