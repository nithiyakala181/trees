Trees - Uses & Specialty

"Trees are the lungs of the Earth"

Neem Tree

Specialty: Known as "Village Pharmacy". Natural purifier and antibacterial.
Uses:
  • Medicine: Treats skin diseases, dental care
  • Agriculture: Natural pesticide for crops
  • Wood: Used for making strong furniture

Coconut Tree

Specialty: Called "Kalpavriksha" - Tree of Life. Every part has a use.
Uses:
  • Food: Coconut, water, oil, sugar
  • Industry: Coir from husk, rope, mats
  • Construction: Leaves for roof, wood for houses

Banyan Tree

Specialty: National tree of India. Lives 200+ years and gives huge shade.
Uses:
  • Environment: Absorbs CO2 and gives lots of oxygen
  • Medicine: Bark and leaves used in Ayurveda
  • Community: Used for village meetings and shade

Teak Tree

Specialty: "King of Timber". Waterproof and termite resistant wood.
Uses:
  • Furniture: Doors, tables, flooring
  • Ships: Used in boat and ship building
  • Export: High value wood for trade

Peepal Tree

Specialty: Only tree that gives Oxygen 24 hours. Sacred in many religions.
Uses:
  • Air: Best for air purification
  • Health: Leaves for asthma, bark for digestion
  • Religious: Worshipped for peace and positivity
